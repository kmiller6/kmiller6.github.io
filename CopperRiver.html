Copper River Salmon

Problem:

Copper River Salmon is a small salmon brand located in Cordova, Alaska. Despite producing some of the best salmon in the world, Copper River only has a small following. The brand was in need of increased awareness.

Solution:

We created a campaign that aimed to do just that, increase brand awaraness. We choose to focus on the Portland market, becasue the north west embraced salmon like nowhere else. We saw Seattle's love of seafood as an example of what portland could become. We created a strategy that involved Print, Digital, and Non-traditional media tactics. I was the lead strategist as well as co-media planner for this campaign. I collected and analyzed data on consumers, and the salmon market, Defined the target market and created strategy that would be successful. As well as researched media outlets to find where to reach customers during times of high receptivity. I ensured our strategy was reflected in our final executions. Client was highly satisfied with the campaign and is considering implementing proposed ideas.
