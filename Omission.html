Omission Beer

Problem:

Omission is a gluten-removed beer, belonging to the Craft Brew Alliance. Omission is truly the best tasting beer that does not contain gluten. Omission is the only gluten free beer to brew with hops and then remove the gluten protein after. Omission was facing large competitors who have more resources. Omission was losing market share in its home state of Oregon.

Solution:

Firmly establish Omission as the best tasting beer that does not contain gluten. This would be done with a national campaign focusing on the eight cities with the highest amount of gluten free food orders. The campaign would implement traditional, digital, and non-traditional tactics. I researched consumers of niche specialty beer markets to gain deep understanding of consumer preferences. I also defined a narrowly tailored target market for gluten free beer and the frequency of which we would reach them. We developed creative ways to reach this audience through a variety of media tactics and events. The client found our tactics attainable and effective.
