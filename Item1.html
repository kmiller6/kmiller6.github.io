Rainbow Sandals

Problem:

Rainbow Sandals produces some of the highest quality sandals available. Rainbows have a reputation of lasting years, in some cases even a decade. Outside of a few markets, Rainbow isn’t a well-known brand. Many people view flip-flops as a cheap disposable product.

Solution:

Highlight Rainbow’s durability. Our assignment was to create print ads that people would react to and reflected a strategy statement. We were also tasked to create an alternative form of advertising. I thought that breaking the world record for most people to surf a wave on the same board would draw attention from the desired audience. Rainbow would sponsor the event and the board would appear to look like one of their sandals.
